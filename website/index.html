About eTickette

eTickette is a Hybrid Smart Queue Management System for STI College Fairview. Built on the principle of "Preparedness over Priority" — your queue number is only issued when you physically arrive and scan at the Kiosk.

  • Reserve a slot online before coming to school
  • Get a QR code — scan at the Kiosk when you arrive
  • Queue number is only issued when you scan at the Kiosk
  • Track your queue live from your phone
