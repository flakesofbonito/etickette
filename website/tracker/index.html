iPhone / iPad — Add to Home Screen
▼ show

On iPhone and iPad, notifications only work when this page is installed as a home screen app. Follow these steps once and you'll receive alerts even when your screen is locked.

1
Open this page in Safari (not Chrome or another browser — iOS only supports notifications from Safari PWAs).
2
Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen (the box with an arrow pointing up).
3
Scroll down in the share sheet and tap "Add to Home Screen".
4
Tap Add in the top-right corner. The eTickette icon will appear on your home screen.
5
Open the app from your home screen, then tap Enable Notifications above and allow when prompted.
Note: If you open this page from the browser tab instead of the home screen icon, notifications won't work on iPhone even after granting permission.
